  • Le Jeu D'échecs
    Chess Championship

    “A warrior is known by his deeds, not his words.”
    - ASTRA



    Description
    Funniche presents "Le Jeu D'échecs - Chess Championship" on June 13th 2021(Stage 1) and June 14th 2021(Stage 2). The competition will be conducted on lichess in two stages. It’s the perfect opportunity to showcase your talent with pieces.

    Event Concluded

    Rules and Guidelines

    Stage 1:

    • Players have to make a lichess account and share the username with us.

    • Each player will get 5 minutes each per match.

    • We will be monitoring the accuracy of players, so cheating with an engine is not allowed.

    • Players will be paired automatically after each match.

    • Scoring criteria: Win: 2 points, Draw: 1 point, Loss: 0 points

    • Leaderboard will be made automatically by the lichess site according to the tally of points.

    • Ranking according to the leaderboard will be considered final and top 16 would be selected for Stage 2.


    Stage 2:

    • Matches will be played according to the Round of 16 format.

    • Each player would have 10 minutes per match.

    • Winners of each match will proceed for further rounds.

    • Each match would be created manually and players would be joining a google meet where their activity would be monitored by an arbiter.

    • In case of a draw, the player who has scored more points in that game wins (Points are displayed in the match itself. The player that is up in points wins)
