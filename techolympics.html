  • Le Jeu D'échecs
    TechOlympics brings to you Le Jeu D’échecs, a Chess competition to get those brain cells up and running!

    Rules and Guidelines:

    • The competition will take place in 2 rounds.
    • Venue : Conference hall, New SAC

    • Round 1:

      1. Day and time : September 3, 9 am to 9 pm.
      2. This will be a Knockouts round, and will take place in slots.
      3. Each slot will consist of 40 players, out of which top 5 in total will be selected per slot.

    • Round 2:

      1. Day and Time: September 4, 9am to 12 noon.
      2. This will be a league round consisting of all the top 5 players across all slots.
      3. Top 3 people will be chosen as winners.

    • FIDE Swiss rules will be applicable.
    • Walk over time will be zero. Participants who are late will not be allowed to participate.
    • The decision of the Organisers will be final in any situation.
    • All the matches will be of the 10 min + 2 sec increment per move format.


    Rules:

    • Each level is timed. Extra time taken (every min) will result in deduction of points.
    • Winning criteria shall be points and total time taken and also time taken per level.

    • Prizes worth Rs. 10K.
