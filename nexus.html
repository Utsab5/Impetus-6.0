WHAT IS NEXUS?

Techniche, the Techno-Management festival of the Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati, shoulders the responsibility of providing exposure to the entire Northeastern region about the technical and managerial aspects of the industry. With the country's young population doting over opportunities in the corporate sector, we take it upon ourselves to lay out a glimpse in this beguiling world.

With that, Techniche presents ‘NEXUS: the corpo-management conference’, the first step into corporate life. The conference spans from 2nd to 4th September, packed with various activities and sub-events. Nexus creates an atmosphere that enables the participants to learn, live and decode the corporate world by interacting with Industry Professionals as well as other participants from across the nation.

WHY NEXUS?

The Conference aims to provide a unique platform, connecting entrepreneurship, business, and management enthusiasts to industrial big-wigs and visionaries.

The Conference boasts itself for providing students with the opportunity to broaden their avenues and learn about the business dynamics of the industry growing at a blisteringly fast pace. The profusion of knowledge and experience shared by speakers, might just be the kickstart you need.

It's also a channel for our industry leaders to stay up to date with today's youth and their aspirations. Apart from this, we also aim to provide all-round development to our participants’ personality and growth with our soft skill subevents, hackathons, and competitions.

What do the participants gain?

  • An opportunity to explore entrepreneurship, management, consulting, finance, and industry.

  • A chance to interact with top executives about their field and how they can get into it.

  • A space to grow their network with like-minded people.

  • A platform to polish their soft skills, confidence and personality.

  • Also, you get hands-on experience with the problems faced in the corporate world, and an overview of blooming new dimensions.

This year’s events lineup

  • Keynotes -

    A comprehensive lecture delivered by accomplished industry leaders followed by a brief QnA session at the end, giving you a chance to learn from their lives and gain valuable insights.

  • Panel Discussion -

    An engaging panel discussion on the topic "Can We or Should We?: The bigger question to address as technology’s role expands in human societies ", conducted by panellists from various industries. This discussion will focus on pressing questions in various industries like AI, telecom, and office culture.

  • Workshop -

    An interactive workshop on multidimensional aspects of personality development, conducted by none other than Mr Yogesh Chabriya. This session would help the audience with not only the corporate world, but also in their personal lives.

  • Hackathon -

    "Hackathons are where your crazy ideas become a reality". With this philosophy in our mind, Nexus brings you a never-before hackathon. The hackathon for this year will consist of two rounds, and enables the participants to think like a company founder. However, unlike other hackathons in this field, we guarantee that the problem statements can be anything but ordinary. Fun is guaranteed and so is Carnage.

  • Interactive Session -

    There's no better way to test your skills than a tight competition. Unleash your creativity and let your imagination go wild with random groups of 3 being made.

  • Networking -

    A chance to connect with the guest speakers and panellists through QnA. This would also be an excellent opportunity for you to connect with a diverse audience from across the nation.

  • Speed Mentoring -

    A series of short focused conversations with the industry professionals of your career interests. Participants can have a direct interaction with the speakers and panellists during an allotted time frame.
