ESCALADE

ASCENT OF GLORY

Escalade is the flagship robotics competition, presented by the robotics module of techniche, is the largest and fastest growing robotics event in the northeast with more than 400 participants and more than 100 teams participating previous year.

Prizes Worth ₹ 1.2 Lakhs

Objective

The aim of the competition is to test the participants skills to make and control robots and to test the robots in specified tasks which mainly focuses on the ability of the robot to climb. It is a team event.

Escalade has successfully completed 10 years with flying colors,which is a testament to its enormous national success.Participants from all over the country compete for the top prize.

Prelims

This time in the 11th edition of escalade we come up with our first event Prelims with exhilarating task in which you have to make a manually/autonomous controlled robot capable of climbing , throwing and holding.Participants will be selected from their performances in prelims which will be held online and they will be promoted in second round(mains) for which will held in IIT Guwahati during Techniche (technical fest of IIT Guwahati).
